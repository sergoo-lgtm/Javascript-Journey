# 🐷 Pig Game


A **2-player dice game** built with **HTML, CSS & JavaScript**.  
Players take turns rolling a dice. Each roll adds to their **current score**.  
If a player rolls a **1**, the current score is lost and the turn switches.  
Players can **Hold** to add the current score to their **total score**.  
The first player to reach **100 points** wins the game. 🎉

---


## 🕹 How To Play

1. Click **🎲 Roll dice** to roll the dice.  
2. The number rolled adds to your **Current Score**.  
3. If you roll a **1**, your **Current Score** resets and the turn switches.  
4. Click **📥 Hold** to add the current score to your **Total Score**.  
5. First player to reach **100 points** wins.  
6. Click **🔄 New Game** to restart.
